1.0 PROPOSED UPS CONFIGURATION

The proposed UPS system consists of modular UPS blocks configured as N redundancy. Each block is sized for the connected IT load. Additional redundant module capacity is not included in the base offer.

2.0 BATTERY SYSTEM

Battery runtime is 10 minutes at full IT load. Battery strings are valve regulated lead acid type. Battery cabinet monitoring is included locally at the UPS HMI.

3.0 ELECTRICAL OUTPUT

UPS output voltage is 415V, 3-phase, 50Hz. UPS system efficiency is 96.5 percent at 50 percent load in double conversion mode.

4.0 STANDARDS COMPLIANCE

The UPS system is designed and tested in accordance with IEC 62040.

5.0 BYPASS AND SYNCHRONIZATION

Static bypass synchronization tolerance is plus or minus 2 Hz. Automatic transfer to bypass is supported subject to source availability.

6.0 MONITORING

UPS monitoring supports Modbus TCP/IP for BMS integration. Alarm points include input failure, output overload, battery low voltage, bypass active, and module fault.

7.0 FACTORY TESTING

Factory acceptance test procedure includes visual inspection, insulation resistance test, functional energization, and alarm simulation. Battery discharge test and transfer-to-bypass witness records will be submitted after site commissioning.

8.0 DEVIATION SCHEDULE

No deviations are declared by the vendor.
