SECTION 3 — PROJECT SCOPE AND CLASSIFICATION
================================================================================

3.1  Tier Classification
     Uptime Institute Tier IV (Fault Tolerant)
     Target: Tier IV Design Certification (TCCF) — submission Q2 2026

3.2  IT Load Capacity (Phase 1)
     Total IT white floor area: 4,200 sq.m
     Installed IT load: 20 MW
     Commissioned IT load: 12 MW (Phase 1A)
     Design power density: 10 kW per rack average, 30 kW per rack peak

3.3  Critical Load Electrical Architecture
     Utility Supply: Two independent 110 kV feeds from KPTCL grid (diversity ≥ 2 km)
     Transformer arrangement: 4 × 5 MVA 110kV/11kV power transformers (2A+2B)
     Medium Voltage: 11 kV double busbar switchgear (A-Bus and B-Bus)
     UPS: 8 × 1.6 MW modular UPS (N+1 per power module, 2N at facility level)
     Battery: VRLA 12V blocks, 15-minute autonomy at full IT load
     Generator: 4 × 3.5 MW diesel generators (N+1 on A-bus, N+1 on B-bus)
     LV Distribution: 415V TPN final distribution via PDU to each rack row
     Package Code: PKG-EL-001 (MV Switchgear), PKG-EL-002 (UPS System), PKG-EL-003 (Generators)

3.4  Mechanical Architecture
     Cooling Philosophy: Chilled water (CHW), rear-door heat exchangers for high-density
     Chiller Plant: 4 × 2.5 MW centrifugal chillers (N+1 per loop, 2N at facility level)
     Cooling Towers: 4 × induced-draft counterflow cooling towers
     CRAH Units: 48 × computer room air handlers (24 per zone, floor-mounted)
     Pump Arrangement: 6 × primary CHW pumps (N+1), 6 × secondary CHW pumps (N+1)
     Design PUE: 1.35 (annualised)
     Package Code: PKG-ME-001 (Chillers), PKG-ME-002 (CRAH Units), PKG-ME-003 (Cooling Towers)

3.5  Fire Suppression
     Data Hall: FM-200 (HFC-227ea) clean agent, VESDA very early smoke detection
     Generator Room: CO2 total flooding
     Office/NOC: Wet pipe sprinkler system per NBC 2016
     Package Code: PKG-FP-001

3.6  Network Infrastructure
     Spine-leaf architecture, 100GbE fabric
     Diverse fibre entry points (North and South trenches)
     ODF rooms at Levels 1 and 2
     Package Code: PKG-IT-001
