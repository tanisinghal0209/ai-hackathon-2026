Kavya, Sanjay,

I've seen Ramesh Khanna's response on the synchronisation window. The vendor's position
is technically incorrect.

IEC 62040-3 does not mandate a specific synchronisation window — that is left to the
project specification. Our specification (REQ-UPS-009) specifies ±1 Hz for this project
specifically because we need reliable bypass transfer under generator conditions where
frequency may be slightly variable.

±2 Hz is indeed common in commercial UPS products, but "common" does not mean "compliant
with our specification." The vendor needs to formally declare this as a deviation.

Sanjay — please note this for the FAT. The synchronisation test should measure the
actual window and compare against ±1 Hz.

Rahul
