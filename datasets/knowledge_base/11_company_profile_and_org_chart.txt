QUALITY POLICY STATEMENT
================================================================================

Tata Projects Digital Infrastructure Division is committed to delivering data centre
facilities that meet or exceed client specifications, applicable codes and standards,
and Uptime Institute Tier certification requirements.

Our Quality Management System (ISO 9001:2015) requires that:
1. All vendor submittals are reviewed against project specifications before approval
2. Non-conformances are reported, tracked, and closed per the NCR procedure
3. Factory Acceptance Tests are independently witnessed for all critical packages
4. Commissioning is conducted by an Independent Commissioning Authority
5. No equipment is dispatched without written dispatch authorisation

Signed: Kavya Reddy, QA/QC Manager — TPL-005
